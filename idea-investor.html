Problem

When tourists come to Jaffna. They are facing many problems to find accommodation, food, and transportation. Because of theses, the number of tourists who visit Jaffna are decreasing day by day. So with the help of this project. I’m expecting that the tourism will be improved in Jaffna.

Solution :-

To increase tourism in Jaffna. We have to solve the tourists’ problems. This will create a curiosity into tourists to visit Jaffna. I’ll hope that this will enhance our tourism in Jaffna and also our local products values will be increased. In our web app, tourists who are wishing to visit Jaffna can log in and they can choose what places they want to visit, where to stay, which food items they want and which transport mode they want (car, van, bus). And also there is a facility that is If you select a place in our app, our app will show the hotels, restaurants, tourists places and transport methods to get that place. And if they finalise their details we can do these facilities to them from their reach up to their return. They have to pay for this. By this, I hope that the tourism will have a development in Jaffna.
